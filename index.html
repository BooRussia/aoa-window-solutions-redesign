Florida expertise

Built for hurricane country.

Florida building codes exist because windows fail first in a storm. We help you understand what's required — and what's worth the investment.

HVHZ & Miami-Dade

The High Velocity Hurricane Zone covers Miami-Dade and Broward. Products here must meet the strictest impact standards in the country — we spec PGT, ES, and CWS lines rated for HVHZ.

Impact vs. non-impact

Impact-rated glass uses laminated layers that hold together when struck by debris. Non-impact may be appropriate inland where code allows — we'll never upsell what you don't need.

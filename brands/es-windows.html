ES (ES Windows)

Imagine Extraordinary

Founded 1983
Headquarters Miami, Florida (U.S. operations)
Parent company Tecnoglass Inc. (NYSE: TGLS)
Founders José Manuel Daes and Christian Daes (Energía Solar)

Overview

ES — formerly marketed as ES Windows — is the residential brand of Tecnoglass Inc., a NYSE-listed manufacturer with more than four decades of glass and aluminum fabrication expertise. The company was founded in 1983 in Barranquilla, Colombia, as Energía Solar before expanding into architectural windows and façades in 1988.

In 2023, ES unveiled a refreshed corporate identity and the tagline "Imagine Extraordinary," reflecting its position as a premium provider of hurricane-resistant and high-thermal-performance window systems. ES products are specified on some of the most demanding residential and high-rise projects in Florida and the Caribbean.

Tecnoglass operates one of the most vertically integrated window manufacturing models in the industry. The Barranquilla campus handles glass processing (including Low-E coating), aluminum extrusion, and finished window assembly — giving ES end-to-end control from raw materials to delivered product.

For Florida homeowners and builders, ES offers a compelling combination of architectural-grade aesthetics, impact certification, and the scale of a global manufacturer. AOA Window Solutions partners with ES to match the right system — from prestige residential windows to commercial curtain wall applications — to each project's performance and design requirements.

Manufacturing & operations

Vertically integrated 6.2 million sq. ft. manufacturing complex in Barranquilla, Colombia, with U.S. offices and showrooms in Miami, Naples, Charleston, New York, and the Florida Panhandle.

Florida & coastal performance

ES is one of the highest-volume impact window brands sold in Florida, engineered specifically for High Velocity Hurricane Zone (HVHZ) requirements and coastal building codes.

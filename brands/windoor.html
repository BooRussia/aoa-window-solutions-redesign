WinDoor

Luxury innovations for windows and doors

Founded 2001
Headquarters Orlando, Florida
Parent company Miter Brands / PGT Innovations family

Overview

WinDoor was founded in 2001 in Florida by Frank Lukens and Russ Traficante with a singular focus: high-performance, impact-resistant windows and doors for the luxury market. From five-star resorts and high-rise condominiums to custom estate homes, WinDoor builds every product to specification rather than pulling from a catalog.

The brand's 8100 series sliding door system is widely specified by architects who need oversized openings — pocket doors, 90- and 135-degree corners, and heights up to 12 feet — without sacrificing Miami-Dade or Florida impact certification. WinDoor holds some of the highest impact ratings in the industry.

Innovations including Diamond Glass and Triton Advanced Laminate push impact technology forward with lighter-weight laminated glass that still meets strict hurricane codes. The refreshed brand identity and tagline "Luxury Innovations for Windows and Doors" reflect WinDoor's position as the ultra-premium coastal living specialist within the PGT Innovations family.

PGT acquired WinDoor in 2016, and manufacturing continues in Orlando, Florida. AOA Window Solutions works with WinDoor on projects where standard impact products won't deliver the scale, sightlines, or architectural ambition the design demands.

Manufacturing & operations

Custom-manufactured in Orlando, Florida, with products engineered and tested for coastal and high-velocity wind environments.

Florida & coastal performance

WinDoor was founded in Florida exclusively for coastal living — producing some of the largest certified impact openings in the industry for luxury South Florida and statewide waterfront projects.

Overview

Custom Window Systems — known as CWS — was founded by Floridians in 1986 with a straightforward mission: build windows and doors specifically for Florida's punishing climate. Nearly four decades later, CWS remains one of the most trusted names in the state's impact and coastal construction markets.

Now a Pella Corporation brand, CWS operates more than 700,000 square feet of manufacturing across Ocala, Miami, Medley, and Jacksonville. Products include vinyl and aluminum impact windows and doors, non-impact systems, and sunroom enclosures — all tested to meet or exceed Florida Building Code and Miami-Dade requirements where applicable.

CWS made history as the first Florida manufacturer to receive an ENERGY STAR® rating from the National Fenestration Rating Council for High Velocity Hurricane Zone windows. That commitment to energy performance alongside storm protection makes CWS a strong specification for homeowners balancing utility costs with hurricane safety.

Real-world performance matters: CWS windows and doors were installed in the "Sand Palace" home in Mexico Beach, which survived Category 4 Hurricane Michael in 2018 while surrounding structures were destroyed. AOA Window Solutions trusts CWS for Florida projects where proven impact performance and dependable delivery timelines are non-negotiable.

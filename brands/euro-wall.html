Overview

Euro-Wall Systems was founded in South Florida with a focus that remains unchanged: build the best large-opening door and glass wall systems for extreme weather. The registered tagline "Superior by Design" reflects a product philosophy centered on engineering, not assembly — every system is built for wind-borne debris, high-velocity hurricane zones, and the daily reality of coastal living.

From its North Port, Florida facility, Euro-Wall manufactures folding (Vista Fold™), multi-slide (Vista Multi Slide™), stacking (Vista Stack™), pivot (Euro Pivot™), and casement window systems. Products meet Miami-Dade and Florida HVHZ standards while offering some of the largest single-panel dimensions available — up to 16-1/2 feet in height or width on select systems.

Thermally broken options including Vista Fold™ and Vista CT™ casement windows support energy code compliance beyond Florida, including California Title 24 and Texas wind-borne debris regions. Despite European-inspired aesthetics, every Euro-Wall product is made in America with automated, high-capacity production for accurate lead times.

AOA Window Solutions specifies Euro-Wall when a project calls for dramatic indoor-outdoor transitions — whole-wall sliding glass, corner folding configurations, or flush-sill multi-slide doors — without compromising the impact rating Florida code demands.

Manufacturing & operations

State-of-the-art facility and showroom in North Port, Florida, producing American-made folding, sliding, stacking, and pivot door systems.

Viwinco Windows

Energy-efficient windows and doors — where production and sustainability go hand in hand

Founded 1982
Headquarters Morgantown, Pennsylvania
Parent company Independent, family-owned
Founders Jack Barnes and Lou Moretzsohn

Overview

Viwinco — short for Vinyl Window Company — was founded in 1982 by Jack Barnes and Lou Moretzsohn in Pennsylvania. Still family-owned and operated three generations later, Viwinco has built its reputation on custom manufacturing rather than mass production: every window and door is built to order at its Morgantown facility.

Unlike many national brands that outsource components, Viwinco has brought critical processes in-house — including glass tempering, laminating, and product development. A dedicated 70,000 sq. ft. glass facility added in 2022 expanded impact glass capacity and improved turnaround times for hurricane-rated products.

The company's lean manufacturing approach delivers industry-leading lead times while maintaining tight quality control. Product lines including Edgemont, Cambridge, Signature Series, and OceanView cover double-hung, casement, sliding, and patio door configurations for both impact and non-impact applications.

For Florida customers in Viwinco's service territory, AOA Window Solutions pairs this made-in-USA craftsmanship with local installation expertise — ideal for homeowners who want custom-sized vinyl windows without the compromises of off-the-shelf big-box products.

Manufacturing & operations

Fully custom vinyl windows and patio doors manufactured at Viwinco's Morgantown, Pennsylvania campus, including an on-site glass facility with laminating and tempering capabilities.

Florida & coastal performance

Viwinco serves the Eastern U.S. from the Canadian border through Central Florida, offering both impact-resistant and non-impact vinyl products engineered for demanding coastal conditions.

Key highlights
